Why go vegan?

Your central hub of anything and everything vegan!
Get informed, find new recipies, and ask questions today

Here are 5 reasons why you should go vegan:

  1. Help Save Lives
    It takes just one person to save 200 animals per year. Animal lives are lives too.
    The most efficient way of becoming part of the solution is to eat plant-based foods.
  2. Become Healthier
    The Academy of Nutrition states that vegans are less likely to develop diabetes,
    cancer, heart disease and high blood pressure. Vegans are also on-average 20-
    pounds lighter than meat eaters are.
  3. Help Feed Others
    Eating meat hurts others around the world just as much as it hurts people. It takes
    up to 13lbs of grain to produce 1 pound of animal flesh! If humans ate that food
    directly, it could help solve world hunger across the globe!
  4. Save the Planet
    A plant based diet is very environmentally friendly. Meat production causes lots
    of pollution and is very wasteful. Adopting a vegan diet is more helpful to the
    environment than switching to a more fuel efficient car!
  5. Your Favorite Celebrities are doing it!
    Lots of popular celebrities have started a plant based diet, some of which include:
    Joaquin Phoenix, Natalie Portman, Ariana Grande, Billie Eilliesh, Alicia Silverstone,
    Casey Affleck, Woody Harrelson and Carrie Underwood!

Banana French Toast

Enjoy your favorite breakfast meal with a vegan twist!
Custard for this French toast breakfast is made using
bananas and rice milk, instead of milk and eggs.


Ingredients:

2 rice bananas
1½ cup rice milk (or preferred non-dary milk)
1 tbs. refined coconut oil
2 tbsp. all-purpose flour
2 tbsp. cornstarch
¼ tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla extract
12 sandwich bread slices

Chickpea Potato Curry With Peas

This stew can be eaten however you want: Hot, cold, right
out the fridge, ect! This recipe is best served with a high
quality curry blend. Serve this dish with basmati rice and
store-bought chutney.


Ingredients:

1 tbsp. coconut oil
1½ cup shallots, diced
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 tbsp. ginger
2 tbsp. mild curry powder
2 tsp. cumin
3 cups vegetable broth
